Mother of Thousands Plant

Kalanchoe laetivirens

Introducing the Mother of Thousands, scientifically known as Kalanchoe laetivirens, a captivating succulent from the Kalanchoe genus native to Madagascar. This plant is also commonly referred to as the Alligator Plant, Mexican Hat Plant, and Devil's Backbone. Renowned for its unique ability to produce numerous plantlets along the edges of its leaves, these tiny plantlets can drop off and root in the surrounding soil, leading to the growth of new plants.This monocarpic plant has an upright growth habit and can grow up to 3 feet tall, making it a striking addition to any indoor or outdoor space
