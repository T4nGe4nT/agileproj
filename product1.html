Super Star

Graptopetalum tacitus bellus

Introducing the succulent sensation: Graptopetalum Tacitus Bellus, fondly dubbed the "super star"! 🌟 Hailing from Mexico and proudly belonging to the Crassulaceae family, this plant is a true standout. Its erect flowers create captivating rosettes of diverse shapes, turning heads with every twist and turn. It's the epitome of effortless growth, adding a touch of magic to any plant collection.And let's talk about those blooms! Imagine vibrant pink and red blossoms stealing the spotlight each early summer. It's a floral extravaganza you won't want to miss!This plant lives up to its superstar status and the best part? It's just as comfortable basking in winter's chill as it is soaking up summer's warmth. Don't miss out on adding this superstar to your plant posse!

  • 🌸Bloom Season:Summer
  • 📈Growth Rate:Slow
  • 📏Mature Size:10in. tall
  • ☀️Sun Exposure:Avoid Full Sun,Partial Shade
  • ⚠️Toxicity: Safe For Humans and pets
