Crassula Pyramidalis

Pagoda Mini Jade

You're not a plant enthusiast if you do not have a Crassula Pyramidalis, and if you want to be one, this plant would be a great start. This plant gets its name from its pyramid-like growth habit. When this plant is exposed to intense sunlight, the leaves may develop red or purple tinges. This beautiful succulent blooms during the mid-spring to summer months. This plant can also grow up to 8 inches and spread outwards as it matures.
