Agave Blue Glow

Agave Ocahui

Peace, Love, and Harmony is essential, so the Agave Blue Glow plant is not only what you want, but is what you need. This amazing symmetrical succulent is best known for adding harmony to any location it is placed. When this plant is backlit by sunlight, it glows! This plant blooms early spring into vibrant yellow flowers.

"Agave Blue Glow" Plant care

  • 🌸Bloom Season: Spring To Early Summer
  • 📈Growth Rate: Slow
  • 📏Mature Size: 2 ft. Tall & 3 ft. Wide
  • ☀️Sun Exposure: Full Sun, Partial Shade
  • ⚠️Toxicity: Midly Toxic For Humans And Pets. Keep Away From Children.
