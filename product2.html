Lola

Echeveria

Meet 'Lola', the dazzling Echeveria hybrid by Dick Wright. This succulent, a blend of E. lilacina and E. 'Deresina', features a pearlescent marble look with rosy undertones. 'Lola' thrives in bright sunlight and well-draining soil, requiring infrequent watering. Known as "Mexican Hens & Chicks," she produces adorable offsets around her base, perfect for forming clusters or transplanting.
