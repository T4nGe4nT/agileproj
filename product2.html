Lola

Echeveria

Meet 'Lola', the dazzling Echeveria hybrid by Dick Wright. This succulent, a blend of E. lilacina and E. 'Deresina', features a pearlescent marble look with rosy undertones. 'Lola' thrives in bright sunlight and well-draining soil, requiring infrequent watering. Known as "Mexican Hens & Chicks," she produces adorable offsets around her base, perfect for forming clusters or transplanting. Easy to propagate from stem cuttings or mature leaves, 'Lola' brings beauty and charm to any garden. 🌿

"Lola" Plant care

    🌸BLOOM SEASON:Early Summer 📈 GROWTH RATE: Slow 📏 MATURE SIZE: 6 in. tall, 4 in. wide ☀️ SUN EXPOSURE: Full Sun, Partial Shade ⚠️ TOXICITY: Safe for Humans, Mildly Toxic (Keep Away from Children)
