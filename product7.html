Aloinopsis Rubrolineata

Impala Lily

Best known for its attractive appearance, this beautiful plant is a one-of-a-kind succulent that is winter-dormant. This plant blooms mid-summer, a beautiful golden with red flowers.

  • 🌸Bloom Season: Winter
  • 📈Growth Rate: Moderate
  • 📏Mature Size: 5 in. Tall
  • ☀️Sun Exposure: Full Sun, Partial Shade
  • ⚠️Toxicity: Safe For Humans But Still Mildly Toxic. Keep Away From Children.
