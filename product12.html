Dudleya Gnoma

White Sprite

If you're looking for a plant that is unique and not seen as much in other gardens, then the Dudley Gnoma is for you! This rare succulent is a summer dormant plant that requires full sunlight for good growth. This flower blooms in the late spring and can grow up to 6 inches tall.

  • Bloom Season: Late Spring
  • Growth Rate: Slow
  • Mature Size: About 6 in. Tall
  • Sun Exposure: Full Sun, Partial Shade
  • Toxicity: Toxic To Humans And Pets.
