Split Rock Succulent

Pleiospilos nelii' mesembs

The split rock succulent, scientifically known as Pleiospilos nelii, is a remarkable plant from the Mesembryanthemaceae family, commonly called "mesembs." Native to South Africa, this egg-shaped succulent showcases two thick, fleshy, gray-green leaves and typically grows up to 4 inches tall and 8 inches wide. Renowned for its unique adaptations to arid environments, the split rock succulent is a standout addition to any collection.

  • 🌸Bloom Season:Mid-Summer
  • 📈Growth Rate:Slow
  • 📏Mature Size:6-10in. tall
  • ☀️Sun Exposure:Full Sun,Partial Shade
  • ⚠️Toxicity: Safe For Humans But Still Mildly Toxic. Keep Away From Children.
