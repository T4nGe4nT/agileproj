Trichodiadema densum

African Bonsai

As soon as you think this succulent is a cactus, it blooms and shows you why it's not. This beautiful African Bonsai is a summer-dormant plant that blooms during the autumn to spring season. It can grow to 4 inches and is the perfect plant for beginner gardeners.

  • 🌸Bloom Season: Autumn To Spring
  • 📈Growth Rate: Slow Growth
  • 📏Mature Size: 4 In.
  • ☀️Sun Exposure: Full Sun
  • ⚠️Toxicity: None
